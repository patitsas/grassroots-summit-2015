Kelley Nayo joined the Qeyno team in April of 2014 and serves as the Chief Of Operations.  After 14 years in the commercial insurance industry that took her to Philadelphia, Baltimore and San Francisco, Kelley owned and operated a diverse array of businesses (retail sales, personal services and residential real estate). This background developed her capacity to be an effective, organized and focused manager, bringing to Qeyno a mix of corporate and entrepreneurial experience.
