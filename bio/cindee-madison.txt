Cindee Madison is a Backend Data Engineer at a small start up in Berkeley, CA. She is currently head of the Women In Tech Inititative at NumFOCUS, where she is also a board member. Previous to her current position she worked at UC Berkeley developing software for brain imaging, where she worked on open source libraries for Brain Imaging including Nipy and Nipype. In addition she has been involved with Software Carpentry, PyLadies, and D-Lab at UC Berkeley, developing courses and teaching computing skills with a special interest in under-represented groups. She received her Masters in Computer Science and Psychology from the University of Minnesota in 2002.
