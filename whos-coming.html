Ascend gives people from under-represented groups an intensive six-week experience in building skills by contributing to open source projects. ClojureBridge aims to increase diversity within the Clojure community by offering free, beginner-friendly workshops for women. Ladies Learning Code is a Canada-wide not-for-profit group working to empower everyone to feel comfortable learning beginner-friendly technical skills in a social, collaborative way. Trans*Code is a hack day in the UK focused on the issues facing trans and gender variant communities.
